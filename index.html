MY PROCESS

MUHAMMAD   SHARIFUZ
PROJECT START TO FINISH
6   STEPS
VERSION   .02
  1. SHFZ PROCESS 01°
    Come Together

    1 Briefing

    Send me a detailed brief with the following information: who you are, core values, business goals, target audience, what you sell, required features, scope of the project, timeframes and deadlines, examples of things you like and everything else you think is important.

  2. SHFZ PROCESS 02°
    With or Without You

    2 Evaluation

    I will take a close look at your brief and assess whether the project meets the terms and requirements of both parties. If there is a match, we will have an online meeting to discuss how to proceed.

  3. SHFZ PROCESS 03°
    Start Me Up

    3 KickOff

    We discuss approaches and concepts to find the perfect solution for your needs. Multiple sessions can be held if necessary.

  4. SHFZ PROCESS 04°
    Paint It Black

    4 Design

    This is where the magic happens. I will work on art direction, concept and design. First a moodboard or 2–3 screens showing the look and feel, then the entire project once the direction is approved.

  5. SHFZ PROCESS 05°
    Working Man

    5 Production

    After approval of the design/mood, the respective screen design is developed, edited or produced. I will update you regularly on the status so you always have a clear insight into the progress.

  6. SHFZ PROCESS 06°
    The Final Countdown

    6 Quality Assurance

    My home turf. After production is complete, we continue to test on multiple browsers and devices to fix bugs — functional and non-functional, until every bug is found, filed and fixed. After your final approval, it ships.

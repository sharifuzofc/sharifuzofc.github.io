My Process — Project start to finish, 6 steps, Version .02

  1. 1

    Briefing

    Send me a detailed brief: who you are, core values, business goals, target audience, required features, scope, timeframes and deadlines, examples of things you like — and everything else you think is important.

  2. 2

    Evaluation

    I take a close look at your brief and assess whether the project meets the terms and requirements of both parties. If there is a match, we have an online meeting to discuss how to proceed.

  3. 3

    KickOff

    We discuss approaches and concepts to find the perfect solution for your needs. Multiple sessions can be held if necessary.

  4. 4

    Design

    This is where the magic happens. Art direction, concept and design — first a moodboard or 2–3 screens showing the look and feel, then the entire project once the direction is approved.

  5. 5

    Production

    Design becomes reality — development, editing, or campaign build. I update you regularly on the status so you always have a clear insight into the progress.

  6. 6

    Quality Assurance

    My home turf. Multi-browser, multi-device testing, functional and non-functional, until every bug is found, filed and fixed. After your final approval, it ships.

FAQ

01. Sharifuz, how much is a website / design / video? (asked by clients)

Let me ask you something first: how much is a car? Depends on which one you want, right? Same here — costs depend on your requirements, scope and features. Your budget defines what I can do for you, and I'm happy to tell you exactly what you'll get for it.

02. How long does a project take? (asked by clients)

Depends on requirements and scope, but typically smaller projects take days to a couple of weeks, and larger ones 1–2 months from kickoff to delivery.
